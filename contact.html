Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a trek?

Simply browse our trips, select your preferred date, and click "Book Now". You can pay via UPI or online payment. For instant booking, message us on WhatsApp!

What's included in the trip cost?

All our trips include transport from Bangalore, meals as mentioned, camping equipment, trek leads, permits, and entry fees. Check individual trip pages for specifics.

Is it safe for solo travelers?

Absolutely! Most of our travelers are solo. You'll be in a group with experienced trek leads and make new friends along the way. We've had 7000+ happy solo travelers.

What's the cancellation policy?

Cancellations 7+ days before: 80% refund. 3-7 days: 50% refund. Less than 3 days: No refund. You can transfer your booking to someone else anytime!
